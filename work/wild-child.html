Wild Child Fools

Wild Child is an American indie pop band from Austin, Texas. They've played at major festivals like, Lollapalooza, Forecastle, Winnipeg Folk Festival, Ottawa Blues Festival, Austin City Limits Music Festival and won Best Indie/Folk Band at South by Southwest. I had the privilege of designing and developing their new site to promote the launch of their new Album, Fools. The site features a full-screen video hero and integrations with Spotify and Bandsintown.
